About the Car

The BMW Infinity redefines the pinnacle of automotive excellence with its cutting-edge powertrain and advanced battery technologies. Designed for unmatched performance, superior range, and ultimate efficiency, Infinity sets a new benchmark in luxury mobility.

Here are several features of BMW Infinity that inform you specifically before you decide anything for this car.

Sport & Strong Car Body

The BMW Infinity platform integrates state-of-the-art powertrain and battery technologies, delivering an unrivaled combination of performance, range, and efficiency. Its innovative module and thermal pack architecture enables faster charging, ensuring greater power and endurance in all driving conditions.

High-Tech Support System

The BMW Infinity instantly connects with multi-device Bluetooth or allows for fast charging with wireless and 36-watt USB-C options. A 22-speaker, 960-watt audio system featuring Active Road Noise Reduction ensures an unparalleled listening experience no matter where you go.

Solid and Elegant Color

The BMW Infinity boasts a drag coefficient of just 0.24 Cd, setting a new standard for aerodynamics in its class. Refined aerodynamic design elements work in harmony with advanced wheels and tires, enabling greater range, sharper handling, and enhanced ride comfort for an unmatched driving experience.
